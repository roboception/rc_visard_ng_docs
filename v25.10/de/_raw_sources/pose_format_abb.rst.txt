ABB pose format
---------------

ABB robots use a position :math:`X`, :math:`Y`, :math:`Z` and a quaternion :math:`Q1`, :math:`Q2`, 
:math:`Q3`, :math:`Q4` for describing a pose, similar to |rc_xxx|
devices. However, the position must be given in millimeters and the quaternion order is as follows:

:math:`q=(\begin{array}{cccc}x & y & z & w\end{array}) = (\begin{array}{cccc}Q2 & Q3 & Q4 & Q1\end{array})`.
