Fruitcore HORST pose format
---------------------------

Fruitcore HORST robots use a position in meters and a quaternion with :math:`q_0 = w`, :math:`q_1 = x`, :math:`q_2 = y` and :math:`q_3 = z` for describing a pose, like |rc_xxx|
devices. There is no conversion needed.
